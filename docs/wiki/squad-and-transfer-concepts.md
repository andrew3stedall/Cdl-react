# Squad and Transfer Concepts

## Squad

A squad is the group of players controlled by a season team. Squad membership is historical, not just current state.

## Temporary rights

A free agency draw win gives the team a temporary right to add the player in that gameweek. If the right is not used before the FPL deadline, it expires.

## Transfers

Transfers are agreed between managers, then approved before they affect squads.

## Loans

Loans temporarily move a player while keeping the player counted against the lending team's squad cap. Loaned players return automatically when the loan ends.

## Negotiations

Trade and loan negotiations happen in app through offers and counters. Private negotiation activity is visible only to involved managers.
